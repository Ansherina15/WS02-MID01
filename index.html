ABOUT SQUIDWARD

Squidward Tentacles is SpongeBob's not so happy neighbor who would do anything to get away from him. Unfortunately for Squidward, he and SpongeBob are also coworkers at the Krusty Krab. This clarinet playing cashier just wants some peace and quiet.

SQUIDWARD CHARACTERISTICS

  • Squidward is portrayed as short tempered, impatient, arrogant, sassy, introverted, condescending, bitter, and misanthropic.
  • Squidward hates his two neighbors' constant disruptive antics. However, the pair are unaware of Squidward's dislike towards them and see him as a friend.
